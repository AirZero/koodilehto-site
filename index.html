About

Codegrove is a cooperative of software professionals located in the Jyväskylä region. We share a common background at the academia. Codegrove provides the infrastructure for effective collaboration and software development work. Codegrove is the glue which allows us to provide various software related services to you.

Some of us operate only during spare time while some focus primarily on customer projects. Our greatest strength lies within varied technical expertise.

We develop and demonstrate this expertise actively by authoring, maintaining and contributing to various open source projects. In addition, many of our members have their own personal software projects out there.

If you want to collaborate with us, please do make a contact. Perhaps we might be able to provide a solution to the problem you are currently dealing with.
