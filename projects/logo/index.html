Koodilehto Logo

The logo of Koodilehto Co-operative consists of a framed tree symbol and the logotype underneath it. The typeface used for the logotype is Latin Modern Sans. This design was selected from a variety of ideas.

The famous Latin Modern family has a beautiful sans serif typeface which was used as the logotype.

First ideas for a logo.

The first tree-themed versions included a more detailed quickly drawn tree stencil against a solid color background. Color and black-and-white versions were examined.

The tree motif comes from the fact that the name Koodilehto actually means 'Codegrove'. The tree, of course, symbolizes the grove pars pro toto.
